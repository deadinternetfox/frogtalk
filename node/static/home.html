Everything You Need
A full chat + social + reels + Frog Channel imageboard + music platform, built for privacy.
🔒

End-to-End Encrypted DMs & Rooms

DMs are encrypted client-side with the Signal Protocol (X3DH + Double Ratchet). Private channels use a per-room AES-256-GCM key derived from a shared secret, with AAD-bound ciphertext and automatic key rotation when a member is removed — the server only ever sees ciphertext.

🌐

Frog Social

A full social feed inside the app — post updates with images, tag friends, follow & block, explore trending posts and channels.

🎞️

Reels

Swipe vertical short videos with hot/new/top sorting, fast autoplay, reactions, reposts, and instant comment threads.

📸

Stories

Share 24-hour stories with your friends or a public audience. Filters, privacy controls, and a built-in camera.

🖼️

Frog Channel Imageboard

Anonymous 4chan-style board with threaded replies, post likes, greentext, image/video/audio uploads, live board chat, and moderator approval tools.

🎬

Watch & Listen Together

Dedicated media channel with a persistent "Now Playing" bar. Watch YouTube, queue Spotify & SoundCloud, react, and vibe together.

🔁

Discord & Telegram Bridges

Link a FrogTalk channel to Discord or Telegram. Messages, photos, videos, voice notes and stickers sync both ways in real time.

📞

Voice & Video Calls

1:1 and group calls over WebRTC P2P. Switch camera, share your screen, or go audio-only mid-call.

🎙️

Voice Messages

Hold to record, release to send. Waveform playback, Telegram-style voice notes that stick around.

Channels & Directory

Public & private text/voice channels, channel images, pinned messages, roles, and a browsable directory.

Friends & Presence

Reactions, Replies & Polls

React with any emoji, quote-reply to any message, and spin up quick polls for group decisions.

Custom Themes

Built for Privacy,
Not Profit

Most chat apps monetize your conversations. FrogTalk is different — your messages are encrypted before they ever leave your device.

  • ✓ Signal Protocol DMs — X3DH + Double Ratchet, forward secrecy per message
  • ✓ Per-channel AES-256-GCM for private rooms with automatic key rotation on ban/kick — server only sees ciphertext
  • ✓ Verifiable Safety Numbers in the in-app 🔒 panel
  • ✓ WebRTC P2P calls — media never touches our server
  • ✓ No analytics, no third-party SDKs
  • ✓ Self-hosted — run your own instance
  • ✓ SQLite — single file backup, you own your data
