Everything You Need
A full chat + social + music platform, built for privacy.
🔒

End-to-End Encrypted DMs

Every DM is encrypted client-side with ECDH + AES-256-GCM. The server only ever sees ciphertext.

🌐

Frog Social

A full social feed inside the app — post updates with images, tag friends, follow & block, explore trending posts and channels.

📸

Stories

Share 24-hour stories with your friends or a public audience. Filters, privacy controls, and a built-in camera.

🎵

Music Together

Dedicated music channel with a persistent "Now Playing" bar. Share YouTube, Spotify & SoundCloud, react, and vibe together.

🔁

Discord & Telegram Bridges

Link a FrogTalk channel to Discord or Telegram. Messages, photos, videos, voice notes and stickers sync both ways in real time.

📞

Voice & Video Calls

1:1 and group calls over WebRTC P2P. Switch camera, share your screen, or go audio-only mid-call.

🎙️

Voice Messages

Hold to record, release to send. Waveform playback, Telegram-style voice notes that stick around.

🏠

Channels & Directory

Public & private text/voice channels, channel images, pinned messages, roles, and a browsable directory.

👥

Friends & Presence

Add friends, see who's online, set a custom status. Real-time presence and typing indicators across the app.

📎

Rich Media

Images, videos, audio, files, GIFs, link previews, and an image lightbox — all inline, all fast.

😄

Reactions, Replies & Polls

React with any emoji, quote-reply to any message, and spin up quick polls for group decisions.

💨

Disappearing Messages

Auto-delete DMs after 1 hour, 24 hours, 7 days or 30 days. Messages vanish on their own.

🎨

Custom Themes

Pick from Cyberpunk, Ocean, Sakura, Retrowave and more — live preview before you switch.

🔔

Real-time Notifications

Instant WebSocket delivery. Browser & mobile push for DMs, mentions, friend requests and posts you're tagged in.

🖥️

Web, Android & Desktop

PWA-enabled web app, native Android APK, Windows .exe, and Linux AppImage / .deb. Your account works everywhere.

Federation Reliability
Keep every FrogTalk node in sync with official release metadata, build hashes, Tor routing preferences, and trust checks.
Ship Once From frogtalk.xyz, Propagate Everywhere
Main site publishes a release feed with package SHA-256 and build hash. Nodes poll the feed, verify legitimacy, prefer onion routes when available, and apply updates safely without leaking onion-only peers.

Central Release Feed

Publish latest version, package URL, SHA-256, and expected build hash from your main domain.

Trust Badges In Network UI

Operators instantly see connected state, official directory status, onion capability, hash match, and legitimacy badges.

Safe Apply Flow

Node downloads update package, verifies checksum, syncs files, and restarts service without drift.

Tor-Enabled Server Handoffs

The app switches users to the node app at /app, preserves handoff tickets when possible, and keeps onion links pointed at the real chat surface instead of the marketing home page.

Not Profit
